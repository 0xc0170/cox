//! <ol>
//! <li>When used as \b temperature \b sensor, connect \b A0 and \b A1 to \b GND, connect \b A2 to \b VCC3.3, because the \b slave \b address in the driver is set to \b 0x1C.</li>
//! <li>When used as \b EEPROM and \b Software \b Write-Protect, apply \b VHI_WP at \b A0 pin and connect \b GND to \b A1 and \b A2 pins. The \b slave \b address is \b 0x31.</li>
//! <li>When used as \b EEPROM and \b Clear \b Write-Protect, apply \b VHI_WP at \b A0 pin, apply \b VDD at \b A1 pin, connect \b A2 pin to \b GND. The \b slave \b address is \b 0x33.</li>
//! <li>When used as \b EEPROM and \b Permanent \b Write-Protect, apply \b VDD at \b A0, \b A1 and \b A2 pins. The \b slave \b address is \b 0x37.</li>
//! <li>Connect the \b SCL and \b SDA pins to the \b master board \b I2C port.</li>
//! <li>Connect the \b Event pin to one \b GPIO pin of \b master board.</li>
//! <li>The pull-up resistor values for a \b 3.3V system ranges \b 9k¶∏ to \b 33k¶∏.</li>
